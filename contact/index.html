Parking

Park in Lot #10, on the first level in a numbered (but not "restricted") space. Be sure to purchase an appropriate permit from one of the dispensers, located near the elevators.


Driving Directions

U.S. 101, from the south: Take the Airport/UCSB exit (Highway 217) off U.S. 101, about eight miles north of Santa Barbara, to the campus East Gate. Veer right at the round-about. Turn left at the first light, into Parking Lot #10.

U.S. 101, from the north: Take Los Carneros exit (Number 107) . Turn right onto Los Carneros Rd. After about 1.5 miles, turn left onto Mesa Road. Continue on Mesa Rd, past 1 stop sign, and 2 traffic signals. Turn right at the 3rd light, into Parking Lot #10.
